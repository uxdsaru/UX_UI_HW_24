In order to help address the needs and concerns of Torontonians, we’ve answered a number of  frequently asked questions below.

NOTE: If your question is not answered below, we recommend contacting the Toronto Public Health Hotline at 416-338-7600, between 8:30am to 8pm. If you are experiencing any symptoms, please contact Telehealth Ontario at 1-866-797-0000, or complete the Covid-19 Self-Assessment, or call 911 for an emergency.

For general questions about the City of Toronto, municipal facilities, and city services, please contact 311 Toronto at 311 (outside city limits: 416-392-2489).
