Latest Update on Vaccination Rollout

Select pharmacies in Toronto that were previously offering the Oxford-AstraZeneca shot to those aged 60 to 64 will remove the upper age
limit and offer the vaccine to anyone aged 60 and older.

Read More Details →

Who is Currently Eligible for a Vaccine?

The following groups are currently eligible for vaccines as of March 27th, 2021:

  • 80 years old or older in 2021
  • 60 to 64 years old in 2021
  • First Nations, Metis & Inuit adults
  • Chronic home health care recepients
  • Long-term care home residents & staff
  • Assisted living residents & staff
  • Retirement home residents & staff
  • First Nations Elder care home residents & staff
  • Essential caregivers for care home residents
  • Essential caregivers for care home residents
  • Personal support workers
  • Front-line health care workers

Think you are eligible? Register for your COVID-19 Vaccine Appointment Now!

The next group(s) expected to be eligible for vaccination as of April 2021.

  • 75 years old or older in 2021
